This campaign is organized by the Fab Foundation - a US non-profit 501(c) 3 organization. Our mission is to provide access to the tools, the knowledge and the financial means to educate, innovate and invent using technology and digital fabrication to allow anyone to make (almost) anything, and thereby creating opportunities to improve lives and livelihoods around the world. Community organizations, educational institutions and non-profit concerns are our primary beneficiaries.

Fab15 Egypt is the annual gathering of Fab Labs organized by the Fab Foundation and Fab Lab Egypt
